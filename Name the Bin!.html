• The Grey Trash Can

    Ready to give him a name?

  • The grey trash can! He’s the OG of the DSNY Recycling team.As a Manhattanite, he knows that NYC never stops running -and neither does waste! As a young can, he’s always been ready to hold on to our trash and never says NO in the name of a clean City. He’s always grateful for New Yorkers trusting him with their garbage.

    One day, after walking around Times Square, a cheery tourist decided to throw away a piece of clothing in a litter basket. When he looked inside, he found that it was a bright orange cape! Always wanting to be as stylish as his fashionable City counterparts, he put it on and soon discovered he had a magical power.

    No, he can’t fly.

    No, he can’t time travel.

    No, he can’t zap things away.

    He realized that he could fight the biggest crime that had hit NYC: waste! He had the power to see items that could be repurposed and saved from going to landfills.

    That was when he decided that he would it make his mission to teach New Yorkers young and old how to reduce waste. In 1986, he decided to begin the DSNY recycling program, and assemble a team of powerful characters who join his fight in reducing waste!

    He wears his #zerowaste belt with pride as he helps NYC reduce, reuse and recycle. Oh, and that cape? He still wears it. Today you can catch him at special DSNY events where he continues to teach New Yorkers of all ages how to be like him - a “waste watcher”!

    When he’s not reducing waste or doing a public appearance, you can catch him partying and dancing his way to zerowaste at Union Square.
